Welcome to the Quiz

This is a simple quiz application.

Each question has to be answered in 10 seconds. If completed within 7 seconds, 3 points are added.
If completed within 4 seconds, 2 points are added, else, 1 point is added.
For 5 or more consecutive correct answers, 3 bonus points are awarded per correct answer.
For 10 or more consecutive correct answers, 5 bonus points are awarded.
For 15 or more consecutive correct answers, 10 bonus points are awarded.
For each wrong answer, 1 point is deducted.

Click the button below to start the quiz.

Test your knowledge and have fun!
